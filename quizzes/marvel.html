💥 5 Legendary MCU Facts

1. The "I am Iron Man" Ad-lib

Robert Downey Jr. improvised the iconic line at the end of the first Iron Man, changing MCU history forever.

2. Vin Diesel’s Special Script

For Groot, Vin Diesel had a script that translated every "I am Groot" into actual sentences so he could get the emotion right.

3. Tom Holland’s Surprise

Tom Holland found out he was Spider-Man through an Instagram post by Marvel. He was so excited he broke his computer!

4. Vibranium Foundation

Vibranium isn't just for Cap's shield; it's the foundation of Wakanda's entire technology and civilization.

5. Stan Lee’s Tradition

Stan Lee appeared in every MCU movie up until 'Avengers: Endgame' as a beloved tradition for all fans.
